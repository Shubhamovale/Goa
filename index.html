South GoaForts, lagoons, and a paddle

The coast turns quiet and green here. A clifftop fort, a hidden lagoon, and the one stretch of sand worth doing nothing on.

MORNING Cabo de Rama Fort — cliff views, old Portuguese stone
AFTERNOON 🛶 Kayaking at Cola Beach lagoon
LATE AFTERNOON Walk over to Butterfly Beach, reachable only by boat or trail
EVENING Drive back up the coast as the light drops
WHY COLA, NOT AGONDA, FOR KAYAKING
  • Calm, enclosed lagoon — flat water, easy paddling
  • Scenery does the work: palm-backed cliffs on both sides
  • Far better photos than the open beach at Agonda
  • Noticeably less crowded, even in season
